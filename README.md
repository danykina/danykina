### 👤 About me

👋 Hi there! I'm **Daniele Chinazzi**, Lead Software & DevOps Engineer @[MOLO17 SRL](https://molo17.com) — Pordenone, IT.

**⏱️ 10+ years across software development, cloud and infrastructure.**

🖥️ My passion for computing started at home — taking apart and building PCs. From there, Arduino and microcontrollers, and later smart home experimentation: Home Assistant, MQTT, Zigbee. It grew into a formal path: a diploma in Computer Science & Telecommunications, then a cloud specialisation at ITS Kennedy in Pordenone with a focus on AWS.

🚀 Over the years I've built experience spanning full stack development to infrastructure automation, expanding into DevOps and cloud architecture. Staying current with evolving technologies is part of how I work — I see AI as a valuable ally, where applicable, to further raise the quality of what I build.
